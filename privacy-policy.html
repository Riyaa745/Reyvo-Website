1. Introduction

Sharpy Innovations Private Limited ("we," "our," or "us"), operating the brand REYVO and its website reyvo.in (the "Website"), is committed to protecting the personal data of every individual who interacts with us. This Policy is governed by the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India and other applicable Indian laws.

We collect only the data that is necessary and proportionate to the purpose for which it is collected.

When you browse or purchase on our full website, we may collect:

  • Identity data: Full name

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies, such as pixels and tags, to enhance your experience and support our marketing activities.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies - required for the Website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies - help us understand how visitors use our Website, such as Google Analytics or similar tools.
  • Marketing cookies - used to show you relevant advertising on platforms such as Google and Meta, such as Meta Pixel or similar tools.
  • Preference cookies - remember your choices and settings on the Website.

When you first visit our Website, you will be shown a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Website.

7. Third-Party Service Providers (Data Processors)

To operate our business, we share your data with carefully selected third-party service providers who process data on our behalf. These may include:

  • Payment gateway(s) - to securely process transactions
  • Email and SMS/WhatsApp marketing platforms - to send transactional and promotional communications
  • Analytics platforms - to measure Website performance and user behaviour, such as Google Analytics or equivalent
  • Advertising networks - to run performance marketing campaigns, such as Google Ads and Meta Ads
  • Logistics and delivery partners - to fulfil and ship your orders
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers - to host and operate our Website and databases

All third-party processors are contractually obligated to handle your data only for the specified purpose, maintain confidentiality, and implement appropriate security measures. We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • With our service providers as described in Section 7, strictly for operational purposes
  • With government or regulatory authorities if required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights
  • In the event of a business transfer - if REYVO or Sharpy Innovations Private Limited undergoes a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the successor entity, and you will be notified accordingly

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes outlined in this Policy, or as required by applicable law.

  • Waitlist data - retained until the launch of the e-commerce website and for a reasonable period thereafter, or until you unsubscribe
  • Order and transaction data - retained for a minimum of 7 years as required under Indian financial and tax regulations
  • Marketing preferences and communication data - retained until you opt out or withdraw consent
  • Technical/device data - typically retained for 26 months in line with analytics platform standards

Once data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

10. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect it from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption on our Website
  • Access controls restricting data to authorised personnel only
  • Secure data handling protocols with our third-party providers
  • Regular reviews of our security practices

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to affect your rights and interests, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required under applicable law.

11. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act, 2023

As a Data Principal, meaning an individual whose data we process, you have the following rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023:

  • Right to access - you may request information about what personal data we hold about you and how it is being processed.
  • Right to correction - you may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to erasure - you may request deletion of your personal data where it is no longer necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to grievance redressal - you have the right to a prompt and effective resolution of any grievance regarding the processing of your data.
  • Right to nominate - you may nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity.
  • Right to withdraw consent - you may withdraw consent for processing at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please write to us at hello@reyvo.in with your request. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with the DPDP Act.

12. Communications and Marketing Opt-Out

By submitting your details on our waitlist or placing an order, you consent to receiving communications from us including:

  • Order confirmations and delivery updates, which are transactional and cannot be opted out of
  • New product launches, offers, and brand news, where marketing opt-out is available at any time

You can opt out of marketing communications by:

  • Clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any email we send
  • Sending an opt-out request to hello@reyvo.in
  • Replying "STOP" to any SMS/WhatsApp message from us

We will process your opt-out request promptly. Please allow up to 7 business days for changes to take effect across all platforms.

13. Children's Privacy

Our Website and products are not directed at individuals under the age of 18 years. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a person under 18, we will delete such data promptly.

If you believe a minor has provided us with their personal data, please contact us at hello@reyvo.in immediately.

Note: The DPDP Act, 2023 imposes additional obligations with respect to processing data of children. We will comply with these obligations fully as our platform scales.
